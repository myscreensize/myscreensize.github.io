Why we built this

In a world of infinite screen sizes and varying pixel densities, developers and designers need a reliable source of truth. My Screen Size was created to bridge the gap between complex hardware specs and easy-to-read data.

Our mission is to provide a clean, ad-free environment where anyone can instantly verify their viewport, resolution, and device capabilities without any technical friction.
